Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis (2022)

## Objective
To analyze Vrinda Store’s 2022 sales data and understand customer behavior to improve sales performance in 2023.

## Dataset
Sales data containing order details, customer demographics, states, product categories, and sales channels.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Charts & Graphs
- Slicers

## Key Insights
- Women contribute approximately 65% of total sales
- Customers aged 30–49 years are the highest contributors
- Top states by sales: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh
- Major sales channels: Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra

## Business Recommendation
Focus marketing campaigns on women aged 30–49 in top-performing states using online platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.

## Project Type
Guided Excel dashboard project created for learning and portfolio purposes
